Strategic Advisory

  • Enterprise Strategy
  • Physician Strategy
  • Payer/Provider Partnerships
  • Specialty Care Strategy
  • Affiliation Strategy
  • Value-Based Care Strategy

Disruptive forces such as consumerism, new entrants, digital technologies, virtual care and value-based payment models are requiring health systems to rethink their strategic positioning and business model and are challenging even the most well-positioned health providers to navigate through the change.

Our assistance involves working with leadership to set strategic direction through defining strategic priorities, ranking and aligning the priorities, outlining the resources required, and developing a milestone-based roadmap to achieve the desired future state.

We help organizations implement the governance, organizational model and cultural change need to realize their strategy.

Financial Advisory

  • Long Range Financial Planning
  • Physician Enterprise Performance Diagnostic
  • Financial Improvement Diagnostic
  • Financial Analysis
  • Turnarounds & Workouts

We leverage our finance experience to support health providers achieve strong, sustainable financial performance. We develop long range financial plans which integrate strategy and finance, linking strategic objectives with measurable financial outcomes. We also analyze the financial implications of strategic options, evaluating and quantifying the financial risk involved in each option and creating scenario-based analysis of potential financial outcomes.

Lastly, our experienced financial advisors understand the key drivers of strong financial performance and can conduct financial diagnostic assessments to outline opportunities to improve financial performance.

Performance Management & Analysis

  • Decision Support & Cost Accounting
  • Service Line Financial Analysis
  • Enterprise Financial Reporting
  • Operating & Capital Budgeting
  • Finance Function Transformation
  • Financial Reporting & Control

Data analytics and other technologies are quickly reshaping the way health providers gain access to actionable insights and critical business intelligence. Using strategically selected key performance indicators—that are produced in real time using business intelligence tools—can significantly improve performance on a wide range of financial drivers.

No longer a luxury, financially successful health providers have processes, people, tools and governance to analyze and benchmark in order to identify and prioritize opportunities on a proactive basis. Modern budget, decision support and cost accounting capabilities, combined with analytics, provide the foundation for organizations that perform well financially.
